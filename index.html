Music-mentorship communities refers to the long established community of tutors and students brought together by the local music tutoring program. ALTO affiliated programs are long established communities which bring high school musicians and younger students together to create music. Students gain inspiration and a glimpse into the next stages of ther musical journey through affordable, consistent private lessons. Tutors are able to sharpen their leadership skills, gain invaluable teaching experience, and give back to the community which built them up as musicians. Consequently, these music-mentorship communities create reciprocal relationships, growing and strengthening the music program as a whole.
